About SOS Infotech & Telecom

Trusted Technology Solutions for a Smarter Digital India

Technology shapes how organisations work and stay connected. At SOS Infotech & Telecom, we support this with secure, steady, and scalable systems across applications, networks, cloud, and infrastructure. Our focus is clear: dependable service, smooth delivery, and solutions that support long-term performance across different sectors.

Pioneering Excellence in Integrated IT Solutions

As a multi-city IT solutions provider, SOS Infocity has expanded its expertise across 14 locations in India, delivering enterprise technology, IoT platforms, cloud migrations, cybersecurity, and mission-critical government digital programs. For decades, we’ve partnered with State departments, PSUs, large enterprises, and development-sector organizations to build robust digital ecosystems that are efficient, secure & future-focused. Our multi-domain experience and strong partner network ensure that every solution is executed with precision - no matter the scale or complexity.
